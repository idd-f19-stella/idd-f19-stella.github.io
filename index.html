About Careers

Graduates of this program will have acquired the skills necessary to be employed in various digital media studios, with careers such as:

  1. Information Architect
  2. Interaction Designer
  3. Mobile Designer
  4. Mobile Developer
  5. Production Artist
  6. User Experience Designer
  7. User Interface Developer
  8. Web Designer
About Students Work & Studios

You’ll combine cognitive science, the history of design, and human-computer interaction to develop a practice that speaks to the issues you care about most. In order to create forward-thinking solutions, you’ll learn to master the following core skills:

  1. Understand behavior and human systems
  2. Master technical and visual interaction for real-world designs
  3. Create, evaluate, and validate physical and digital prototypes
  4. Demonstrate work through presentations and user interfaces
  5. Identify opportunities and produce original design responses
About How to apply

Our applicants are often just as interested in compelling visual design as they are in detailed documentation through wireframes and sketches. Whether they’re reinvigorating their careers or approaching interaction design for the very first time, they care deeply about making the world a better place.
